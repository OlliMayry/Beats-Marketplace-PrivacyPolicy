BeatsMarketplace Privacy Policy
Welcome to BeatsMarketplace, a platform dedicated to music enthusiasts. Before you create an account, please take a moment to review our privacy policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

By clicking the "Accept Rules" button during registration, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
Information We Collect
During the registration process, we collect the following information:

- Email address - Username - Phone number (optional) - Profile image (optional)

Additionally, when you interact with the app, we may collect:

- Beats data (audioUrl, beatID, beatName, bpm, etc.) - Chats and messages data - Contacts data - User interactions and preferences
How We Use Your Information
Your information is used for the following purposes:

1. Authentication: To register and log you into your account securely.

2. Profile Management: To display your profile information to other users.

3. Beat Uploads: To store and showcase beats uploaded by you.

4. Communication: To facilitate messages and contacts between users.

5. Analytics: To analyze user behavior and improve our app's performance.
Profile Management
Your profile information is intended for display to other users within the BeatsMarketplace platform. However, certain sensitive details, such as your email address, password, and phone number, are never disclosed to other users.

The information visible to other users includes:

- Username: Your chosen display name within the BeatsMarketplace community. - Profile Image: An image you may choose to upload for personalizing your profile. - Total Downloads: A count of the total number of downloads your beats have received.

Rest assured that your email address, password, and phone number remain confidential and are never shared with other users. We prioritize the security and privacy of your sensitive information.
Data Security
We take the security of your data seriously. Here's how we secure it:

- Firestore Security Rules: Access to data is restricted based on user authentication.

- Firebase Storage Rules: Secure storage access based on user authentication.

- Password Encryption: Passwords are securely hashed before storage.
Your Rights
You have the right to:

- Access, correct, or delete your personal information.

- Manage your privacy settings.

- Contact us for assistance or information regarding your data.
Data Deletion
If you decide to delete your account, all your associated data, including uploaded beats, messages, and profile information, will be permanently deleted from our servers.
Updates to Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. Please check this page regularly for updates.
Contact Information
If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to us via the contact page or email us at beats.marketplace51@gmail.com.

By accepting the rules with registration progress, you acknowledge that you have read and understood our privacy policy.
